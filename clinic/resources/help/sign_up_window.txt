<p style="text-align: justify;"><strong>Sign up window</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">In this window you can sign up as a new patient by filling in the necessary information.</p>
<ul>
<li>Passport: series and number (separated by a whitespace) or just number</li>
<li>Date of birth: dd.mm.yyyy</li>
<li>Full name: name, surname and maybe patronimic (separated by whitespaces)</li>
<li>Place of residence: country name, city name, street name, building number, apartment number (separated by whitespaces)</li>
</ul>
